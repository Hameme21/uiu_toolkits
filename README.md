## About UIU Toolkits
UIU Toolkits is an all-in-one academic utility platform engineered explicitly for Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) students at United International University (UIU). Navigating university life requires juggling complex class schedules, tracking prerequisites, avoiding final exam clashes, and keeping an eye on tuition installments—tasks that can quickly become overwhelming.

This toolkit bridges those gaps by providing a localized, lightning-fast dashboard that works fully offline as a native app. It takes the friction out of trimester planning so you can focus on what truly matters: mastering your code.

Key Features Built For You:
Exam Clash Solver: Visualize your final exam slots based on the official template and instantly detect heavy same-day pressures or overlapping critical clashes.

Dynamic Section Planner: Drag, drop, and draft your ideal weekly class schedule on a clean timeline grid before advising begins.

Smart CGPA Calculator: Seamlessly apply accurate UIU grading scales, calculate targeted roadmaps, and effortlessly factor in complex retake rules.

Tuition Fee Estimator: Plan your trimester budget instantly by factoring in cost-per-credit settings, university waivers, and multi-trimester projections.
